Technology

Technology has been evolving and changing rapidly over the course of centuries. It has and will continue to rapidly shape our day-to-day lives and will have a great impact in many parts of our society. Already, we have been viewing the impact of technology, with technology being used for small tasks such as viewing the news to grand tasks such as using technology in the process of surgery. The existence of technology has transformed the lives of humans and has made completing many tasks much more efficient and effective. With the COVID-19 pandemic, the usage of technology has only increased in both education and workplaces all around the world. Technology currently has a massive place in all societies all around the world and can only improve by leaps and bounds from here on out.

1927

In 1927, the first electronic television was created in San Francisco on September 7th. This television system was created and and designed by Philo Taylor Farnsworth, who at the time was a 21 year old inventor.
